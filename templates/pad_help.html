To center, justify align left / right a text, write %center|justify|right|left% your text %endalign%

Elements

You can make:
  • Bold (*)
  • Italic (_)
  • Cross (~)
  • Underline (--)
  • Title (|)
  • Quote (>)
  • Dot list (.)
  • Square list (-)
  • Check list (+)
  • Numerate list (=) + (.)
  • Centered (%center% - %endalign%)
  • Justified (%justify% - %endalign%)
  • Right align (%right% - %endalign%)
  • Left align (%left% - %endalign%)
with text.

Examples

  • *bold text\*
  • _italic text\_
  • ~crossed text\~
  • --underlined text\--
  • |title\|
  • >quoted text\>
  • .dot list element\.
  • -square list element\-
  • +check list element\+
  • = .numerated list element\. \=
